About Us

Working With Us | Commitment to the Highest Quality

Our attention to detail and years of experience will make your dreams become a reality.

Over 20 years ago, Color Unlimited Painting began with the mission of bringing high-end customers in Mesquite, Texas the best solutions in the industry. We assist both homeowners and businesses in making their painting projects easier and more convenient. Our company takes pride in providing our clients with the personalized service they deserve. Color Unlimited Painting is focused on the clients’ needs, which allow us to pay attention to every detail of every project. When you work with us, you can be sure that what we promised we would deliver, will, in fact, be what we provide you at the end of the project. All of your needs are a priority to our team, which is why you can expect the highest quality solutions. Our entire team at Color Unlimited Painting is always thinking about how we can make your experience with us better. From how we communicate with you to the impeccable finishes we deliver; we wish that you feel comfortable with us from start to finish. On top of it all, we want you to feel respected through the entire painting project.
